Notes

c-icap
Current AV failure mode is fail-{{ clamav_options.clamav_fail_mode }}. Fail-open renders Squid bypass=on and c-icap virus_scan.PassOnError on so browsing continues unscanned during scanner outages; fail-closed renders bypass=off and PassOnError off so affected transfers fail instead.
The Enable button changes the proxy's AV policy only. It does not supervise or start the clamd daemon or the AV c-icap service.
Upload and download scanning are independently configurable inside this policy. The top-level enable button is a convenience control that flips both scan directions together without changing the rest of the file-blocking policy.
AV scanning behavior is controlled by the local c-icap service inside the container (for example /etc/virus_scan.conf), which forwards scan requests to the configured remote ClamAV daemon at CLAMD_HOST:CLAMD_PORT.
